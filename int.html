Butwal is a vibrant and rapidly growing city located in the Lumbini Province of Nepal. It serves as a significant commercial, industrial, and educational hub in the western region of the country. Situated at the confluence of the Terai plains and the hilly region, Butwal enjoys a strategic location that has historically made it a crucial point for trade and transportation.
Connectivity and Infrastructure The city's infrastructure is well-developed, with good road connectivity that links Butwal to major cities like Kathmandu and Pokhara, as well as to the Indian border. The Siddhartha Highway and the East-West Highway are crucial routes that pass through Butwal, enhancing its accessibility. Additionally, the nearby Gautam Buddha Airport in Bhairahawa provides air connectivity, facilitating domestic and international travel.
